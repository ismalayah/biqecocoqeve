How many times was Barbara Walters married?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

A broadcast journalist and television personality from the United States.

Walters was a renowned television anchor who was well-known for her skill at conducting interviews.

She hosted shows like Today, ABC Evening News, 20/20, and The View. From 1951 until her retirement in 2015, Walters worked as a journalist.

How many times was Barbara Walters married?

Barbara Walters wed three different men on four separate occasions. Business executive and former Navy lieutenant Robert Henry Katz was her first spouse. On June 20, 1955, they tied the knot at The Plaza Hotel in New York City.

After eleven months, in 1957, the marriage was declared null and void. Lee Guber, a theatrical producer and theater proprietor, was her second spouse. They got hitched on December 8th, 1963, and got divorced in 1976.

Jacqueline Dena Guber was adopted by the couple after Walters experienced three miscarriages (born in 1968, adopted the same year).

CEO of Lorimar Television Merv Adelson was her third spouse. They got hitched in 1981 and got divorced in 1984. They got remarried in 1986 and went through a second divorce in 1992.
