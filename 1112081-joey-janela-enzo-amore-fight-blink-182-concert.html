Joey Janela, Enzo Amore Get Into A Fight At Blink 182 &amp; Lil Wayne Concert

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, May 19, 2024

Update: Enzo released some footage from the encounter, but no physical altercation can be seen.

Joey Janela and Enzo Amore reportedly got into a fight at a Blink 182 and Lil Wayne concert last night, and then continue to throw some verbal jabs on Twitter.

Both were in attendance for the concert at the PNC Bank Arts Center in Holmdel, New Jersey, and Janela tried to introduce himself to Enzo. According to both accounts, Janela approached Enzo, who slapped his hands down and things escalated from there.

Not a work, So i saw the dude and walked up and said “Hi I’m Joey Janela” at the blink 182 show and we proceeded to have (I’m not lennox lewis) to have the shittiest fist fight of the year, I’m not a pussy but we had fun!

While it might be the first time the two had a physical altercation, they have clashed on social media in the past. Janela and Enzo had issues in April after Janela called out Enzo for making a mockery of the business and challenged him to a fight under GCW’s Bloodsport banner.
